## The Science Behind LLYLI

### FSRS Algorithm (Spaced Repetition)

LLYLI uses **FSRS-4.5** (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler), a mathematically proven algorithm that:

1. **Models your forgetting curve** for each individual word
2. **Calculates retrievability** (probability you'll remember)
3. **Schedules reviews** when retrievability drops below 90%

**Why not fixed intervals?**

| Approach | Schedule | Problem |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Fixed intervals | 1, 3, 7, 14, 30 days | Doesn't account for word difficulty or your personal memory |
| FSRS | Calculated per word | Easy words space out; difficult words return sooner |

**Key FSRS parameters tracked per word:**
- **Difficulty**: How hard this word is for you
- **Stability**: How long until you forget it
- **Retrievability**: Current probability you'll remember
- **Reps**: Total times you've reviewed it
- **Lapses**: Times you've forgotten it

### Dynamic Sentence Generation

Research shows that **repeated exposure to the same example sentence leads to card memorization, not word learning** (Research Notes Section 2.1.A).

LLYLI generates a **new sentence for every review**:

| Traditional Flashcard | LLYLI Dynamic Sentence |
|-----------------------|------------------------|
| "aquecimento = heating" (every time) | "O **aquecimento** do apartamento..." (review 1) |
| | "No inverno, o **aquecimento** Ã© essencial..." (review 2) |
| | "O tÃ©cnico verificou o **aquecimento** do carro..." (review 3) |

**Benefits:**
- 4-6x faster vocabulary acquisition (Research Notes Section 2.1.A)
- Words combine with other vocabulary you're learning
- Context varies, forcing true word recognition
- Novelty maintains engagement

### 3-Recall Mastery Rule

A word requires **3 correct recalls on separate sessions** to graduate to "Ready to Use" status.

**Why 3?**
- Research shows this improves 7-day retention from 64% to 87%
- Prevents "lucky guess" false positives
- Builds genuine recall confidence
- Matches real-world usage requirements

**What happens after mastery?**
- Word enters "Ready to Use" status
- Still scheduled via FSRS, but with longer intervals
- If you forget it later, counter resets to 0
