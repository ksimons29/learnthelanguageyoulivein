## Appendix: Session Types

### Daily Review
Your main learning session. 10-20 minutes of spaced repetition with fresh sentences combining your due words.

### Boss Round (After Daily Goal)
Completed your 10-word daily goal? Challenge yourself with a 90-second speed round of your 5 most difficult words. Optional but satisfying.

### Bingo Board
Track daily achievements: review streaks, exercise variety, category exploration. Small wins that add up.
