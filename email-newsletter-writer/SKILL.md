# Email Newsletter Writer

## Core Principles

1. **Subject line is everything**: 50% of email success
2. **One goal per email**: Single CTA or message
3. **Scannable**: Most readers skim
4. **Mobile-first**: 60%+ read on mobile

## Email Templates

### Welcome Email
```
Subject: [Welcome/You're in] + [immediate value]

Hi [Name],
[One sentence: what they have access to]
[2-3 bullet quick wins]
[Single CTA button]
```

### Status Update

[What's happening]

**Key details:**

### Newsletter Structure
```
## Main Story
[Hook + Body + CTA]

---

## Quick Hits
- [Item with link]
- [Item with link]

[Footer]
```

## Subject Line Formulas

- Question: "Ready to [outcome]?"
- Number: "[X] ways to [benefit]"
- How-to: "How to [goal] in [timeframe]"
- News: "New: [feature] is here"
- Curiosity: "[Unexpected claim]..."

**Avoid:** ALL CAPS, !!! spam triggers
**Length:** 30-50 chars for mobile

| Context | Tone |
